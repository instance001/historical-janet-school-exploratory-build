**Restatement**: "Invent Your Own Pattern" involves creating a sequence using colors, shapes, or numbers that repeats in a specific order. The goal is to determine the continuation of this pattern.

**Steps**:
1. **Choose Elements**: Select elements such as colors (e.g., Red, Blue), shapes (e.g., Circle, Square), or numbers.
2. **Set the Order**: Arrange these elements in the sequence you want them to follow.
3. **Extend the Sequence**: Continue the pattern by repeating the order until you are asked for the next element.

This approach allows for understanding and predicting the continuation of simple repeating patterns, which is foundational for recognizing more complex sequences later.
